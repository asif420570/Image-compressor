Your Essential Compression Questions Answered (FAQ)

Q: What's the best way to compress an image without losing quality?
A: While all effective compression is "lossy," our tool uses a smart algorithm to reduce file size significantly with almost no visible drop in quality. It finds the optimal balance, making it perfect for photographers and designers who need crisp, clear images.
Q: How does this tool compress PNG files with transparency?
A: We designed our optimizer to fully preserve PNG transparency. You can safely reduce a PNG file's size for your website or design project without losing the transparent background, which is a common problem with other tools.
Q: What makes a "client-side" image compressor safer?
A: "Client-side" means the entire compression process happens on your own device using your browser's power. Your files are never sent to an external server. This guarantees your privacy and makes Simple Compress Go the most secure image compressor online.
Q: How exactly does reducing image size help my website's SEO?
A: Page speed is a major Google ranking factor. Smaller images lead to faster load times, which improves your Core Web Vitals score. This reduces your bounce rate and signals to Google that your site offers a great user experience, boosting your rankings.
